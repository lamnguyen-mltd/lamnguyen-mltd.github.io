2019-05-08: Won Dissertation Award for my PhD thesis.
I have won the 2019 P.C. Rossin College of Engineering and Applied Science Elizabeth V. Stout Dissertation Award for my PhD thesis "A Service System with On-Demand Agents, Stochastic Gradient Algorithms and the SARAH Algorithm". Thank for the supports of my PhD advisors Katya Scheinberg and Martin Takáč, and my previous advisor Alexander Stolyar. Also, thank for the helps of Marten van Dijk, Luis Vicente, Frank E. Curtis, and George Wilson.

2019-04-21: Two papers were accepted to ICML 2019.
Our papers "Characterization of Convex Objective Functions and Optimal Expected Convergence Rates for SGD" and "Certifying Robustness of Neural Networks with a Probabilistic Approach" have been accepted to ICML 2019. Thank for my collaborators Marten van Dijk, Phuong Ha Nguyen, Dzung Phan, Lily Weng, Pin-Yu Chen, Mark Squillante, Akhilan Boopathy, Ivan Oseledets, and Luca Daniel.
